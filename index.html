Movie Reviews App

The Movie Review App is a web application designed to help users discover new movies and share their thoughts through reviews. The app allows users to browse a vast collection of movies, read detailed information about each film, and explore reviews written by other users. Additionally, authenticated users can contribute to the community by writing their own reviews for movies they have watched. The app features rate limiting and pagination to ensure fair usage and optimal performance.
